Email Marketing

How many times per day do you check your emails? And how many of them do you read? Making an impression with email marketing isn’t easy – there’s a lot of competition in that cluttered inbox. But with some inspiring subject lines, brilliant content, a beautiful email template, and a little database management, you’ll soon start making waves.

Few types of marketing are more direct than an email. Email marketing can be used to communicate a range of important messages, such as promotions, sales, key messages, welcome to new customers, discount codes, special events, gentle reminders, and thanks for the business. As well as being informative, in most cases, your email will have a call to action too. What’s the next step you want your customer to take? Email campaigns act as a gateway to get new customers, existing customers, or old customers back on track and to direct them to your website.

One of the most important factors in email marketing is reaching the right people at the right time. Tailoring the audience of your email campaigns is essential, so the message you send is relevant and timely in your customer’s eyes. A blanket approach seldom gets the best results.

Then there are the technical hurdles. We can offer guidance on avoiding spam filters, plus our HTML emails are crafted to display perfectly on tablet, mobile, and desktop. Don’t forget that there are legal implications, too, regarding whom you can contact and when, so don’t get caught out. Want to pick our brains about email marketing? We’d love to chat.
